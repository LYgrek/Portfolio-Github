About Me

Ouroboros

The creation of this website is part of a group project
carried out for my studies, in collaboration with Gabriel Carneiro,
Maïna Rahal, and Meysem Essafi. The project guidelines were to create
a website showcasing a specific typography. In our case,
we chose to use the Ouroboros typeface through various designs.
We decided to integrate it into a universe that is both
astrological and astronomical.
